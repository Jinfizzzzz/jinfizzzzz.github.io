As a consultant, I approach problems through a cross-functional and multidisciplinary lens.

I believe the problems worth solving are rarely defined by a single cause or a single point of view.
In complex organizational and business contexts, outcomes are shaped not only by structure and data, but also by people and the situation they operate in.

How I work

Rather than jumping straight to solutions, I start by clarifying where perspectives diverge and where decision-making gets stuck.
I typically begin with an operational view—processes, system structure, and data. When needed, I expand the lens to include organizational dynamics and decision-making context to form a more complete picture.
In some cases, I strengthen the analysis with an IT- and data-driven approach to reframe the problem and set clear criteria for discussion and evaluation.
Ultimately, my goal is to help teams see the same problem in the same language—so they can make practical choices and translate them into execution.
